Privacy Policy — Horus Detect - Demo

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Horus Detect - Demo is a demonstration application for acoustic drone detection. This Privacy Policy explains what data is accessed or used by the application and for what purpose.

1. Data collection

Horus Detect - Demo does not collect personal data.

The application uses the device microphone only to analyze ambient sound in real time. The audio stream is processed locally on the device to display an indicative probability level of possible drone presence.

2. Microphone use

Microphone permission is required to enable acoustic detection.

The microphone is used only when the user manually starts detection in the application. Detection only works while the application is open in the foreground.

3. Audio recording

Horus Detect - Demo does not record audio.

The application does not store audio files, does not create an audio history, and does not allow audio captured by the microphone to be exported.

4. Local processing

Acoustic analysis is performed locally on the device. The detection model is embedded in the application.

No audio stream is sent to a server for analysis.

5. Data sharing

Horus Detect - Demo does not share audio data with third parties.

The application does not sell user data and does not transmit personal data to advertisers or commercial partners.

6. Internet access

The application may declare network permission for technical compatibility or future update purposes, but this demo version does not transmit microphone audio to any server.

7. Diagnostic data

The current version does not intentionally collect diagnostic data, behavioral analytics, or user identifiers within the application.

Some technical information may be processed by Google Play or the Android system as part of the normal distribution, installation, and operation of Android applications.

8. Security

The application limits data processing to what is strictly necessary for local acoustic detection. No audio recording is stored by the application.

9. Target audience

Horus Detect - Demo is not specifically intended for children.

10. Application limitations

Horus Detect - Demo is a demonstration application. The displayed results are indicative and may vary depending on the device, ambient noise, distance, environment, and conditions of use.

The application is not a certified professional security, surveillance, or alerting system.
