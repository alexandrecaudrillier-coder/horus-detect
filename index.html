Horus Detect - Demo

Horus Detect - Demo is a demonstration Android application for local acoustic drone detection. It uses the phone microphone to analyze ambient sound in real time and display an indicative probability level of possible drone presence.

The demo version works only while the application is open in the foreground. It does not record audio, does not store audio, and does not transmit microphone audio to a server.

Privacy Policy

Horus Detect - Demo is a technical demonstration. It is not a certified professional security, surveillance, or alerting system.
